About

Design that earns its place on the page.

I'm Lata Devi, a graphic designer based in Punjab working with brands, publications and startups who want their visual identity to feel considered — never generic. Over the last several years I've built identity systems, packaging, editorial layouts and digital interfaces for clients across India and beyond.

My process starts with a real brief: audience, tone, constraints — then moves into type, grid and colour. The result is design with a point of view, built to hold up under scrutiny.
